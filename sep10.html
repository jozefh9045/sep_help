SEP 10

Processing

Processing is a coding language based off of JavaScript. Here students will be able to put shapes onto the screen through the code, have interactivity between the user and the program through the mouseand buttons, arrays, and objects, both of which are variables that hold multiple variables.

Makey Makey

The Makey Makey unit will introduce SEP students wiring. Students will make buttons and circuts and use them to send messages to the computer from the real world to do something; like changing a variable or making a sound, for example.

Arduino

Arduino. Like Makey Makey, Arduino is in the phyisical world, but this time, students will get the real-world lights to change with code from the computer.
